# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be welcoming, patient, and useful for beginners and experienced contributors alike.

Participants are expected to:

- be respectful and constructive
- assume good intent
- give feedback on ideas and behavior, not on personal traits
- help keep discussions accessible to newcomers

## Expected Behavior

- Use clear, professional, and kind language.
- Be open to feedback and correction.
- Focus on what helps users and contributors succeed.
- Respect different experience levels, identities, and communication styles.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- personal attacks or insulting language
- deliberate disruption, trolling, or bad-faith engagement
- sharing private information without permission

## Scope

This applies to project spaces, issue discussions, pull requests, reviews, and other community interactions connected to this repository.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that do not align with this code of conduct.

Repeated or severe violations may lead to temporary or permanent exclusion from the project community.
